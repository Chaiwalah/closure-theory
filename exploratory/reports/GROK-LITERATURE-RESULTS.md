### 🎯 Tang et al. (2025) — arXiv:2507.08245
**61 galaxies at z=9-14: EWs 2-3× larger than z=6-9**
- CIII], Hβ, Hγ all show extreme EWs at highest z
- **OUR ANGLE**: If the medium eats CONTINUUM preferentially at shorter wavelengths (blue-preferential), EW = line flux / continuum flux would INCREASE because the denominator shrinks. The "extreme EWs" might be partially an artifact of continuum depression, not intrinsically extreme line emission.
- **ACTION**: Check if the EW enhancement is wavelength-dependent (bluer lines more enhanced)
