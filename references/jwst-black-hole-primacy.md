### Little Red Dots (LRDs)
- New class of objects discovered by JWST
- Compact (few light years across), red emission
- ~1/3 of all active objects at high z are LRDs
- Believed to be active galactic nuclei / massive BHs with surrounding gas
- Challenge co-evolution models completely
